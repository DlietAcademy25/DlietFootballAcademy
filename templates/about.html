About Us

Dliet Football & Tigrigna Academy is dedicated to promoting culture and heritage through sports and educational programs. We strive to support our community by providing opportunities for growth and development. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where individuals can thrive and achieve their full potential. We believe in the power of sports and education to bring people together and foster a sense of community.

Our History

Founded in 2025, Dliet Football & Tigrigna Academy has been a cornerstone of the community, offering a variety of programs that celebrate our rich cultural heritage and promote physical fitness. Our journey began with a small group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a difference in the community. Over the years, we have grown and expanded our programs to include a wide range of activities that cater to all age groups.

Our Staff

Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to providing the best possible experience for our participants. Meet our staff and learn more about their backgrounds and expertise. Their commitment and passion are evident in everything we do, from our cultural preservation projects to our support services for new immigrants. Together, we strive to build a resilient and vibrant community for future generations.
